Get top IPs from an access log, assuming the Common Log Format:
    awk '{ print $1 }' 2014-07-14_15.access.log | sort | uniq -c | sort -rn

Get the top URL:
    awk '{ print $7 }' 2014-07-14_15.access.log | sort | uniq -c | sort -rn

Get IPs requesting pages returning a 400:
    awk '( $9 ~ /400/)' 2014-07-14_15.access.log | awk '{ print $1 }' | sort | uniq -c  | sort -rn

Etc.
